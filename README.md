# Hearth

A free, private baby tracker. No accounts, no ads, no cloud.

Hearth tracks sleep, feeds, diapers, medicine, and pumping. Everything stays on your device. Install it as a PWA and use it offline.

## Track

- **Sleep** — start, end, and quality
- **Nursing** — side, duration, and time
- **Bottles** — contents and amount
- **Diapers** — wet, dirty, or mixed
- **Medicine** — custom medicines with dose and time
- **Pumping** — side, amount, and time

## Why local-first

Your data lives on your phone. Hearth never sends it anywhere. No server, no login, no subscription. The app works offline and loads instantly.

## Share with caregivers

Invite other caregivers to view and log entries together. Everyone sees the same timeline.

## Install

Open the site on your phone and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
